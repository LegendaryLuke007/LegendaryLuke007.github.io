# WORK EXPERIENCE

## Summary

My work experience has quite a spread. I have worked anywhere from farming, undergraduate research,
construction, slinging coffee in a coffee hut, to IT Field Representative. My wide range of experiences
have allowed me to have a wide breadth of knowledge and abilites from organization and product
management to customer service and statistical Analysis.

### Vicinity IT Field Representative (2024-Present)

#### JOB DESCRIPTION:

As an IT Field Representative I am incharge of all the in person trouble shooting and customer relations
in the Fairbanks Area. Vicinity, being an MSP and having a wide range of clients, had me going from working
on some networks in Healthcare, to small businesses, to some more industrial systems up in Prudhoe Bay Alaska. I
Spend most of my time troubleshooting network connectivity, organizing the deployment and maintenance of 
Windows machines, and communicating with clients about potental hardware/software purchases and upgrades.

#### SKILLS ACQUIRED:

- Writing and documenting IT issues through a ticketing system
- Providing a quality customer experience while diagnosing and fixing IT related issues
- General troubleshooting of Networks
- Setting up Corporate Windows Managed Machines
- Modifying user privledges and changing executional abilities
- Setting up OneDrive and Shared Drives within an organization
- Installing basic Network hardware (firewalls, Network Monitoring hardware, Ethernet, Modems, etc.)
- Setting up and troubleshooting Network devices (printers, cameras, etc.)
